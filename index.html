Innate Perspectives FAQ

Is this coaching or therapy?

No. This work is not therapy, counselling, coaching, or emotional processing. The focus is functional recalibration — restoring internal coherence so you can operate clearly under pressure.

How is this different from leadership coaching?

Coaching focuses on insight and strategy. This work focuses on internal access when pressure is high and thinking narrows.
